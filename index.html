Mengjie (Rainbow) is a singer, and a member of Rocket Girls 101

Rocket Girls 101 on Wikipedia

Get to know Mengjie

Mengjie was born on June 19, 1994. She is from Zhejiang, China.

About her debut

Mengjie made her debut with Rocket Girls 101 on 23 June 2018. This group was formed by Tencent through the 2018 reality show Produce 101 China on Tencent Video.
